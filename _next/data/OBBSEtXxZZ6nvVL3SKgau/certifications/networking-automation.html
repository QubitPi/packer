HashiCorp Certified:

Consul Associate (003)

Product version tested:Consul 1.11 or higher

The Consul Associate 003 is an upcoming certification for site reliability engineers (SREs), solutions architects (SAs), DevOps professionals, or other cloud engineers who know HashiCorp Consul fundamentals and have the skills to build, secure, and maintain Consul. Candidates will be best prepared for this exam if they have professional experience using Consul in production, but performing the exam objectives in a personal demo environment may also be sufficient. This person understands available Consul Enterprise features and can differentiate between Enterprise and Community Edition.

To renew your Consul Associate certification, you will need to take and pass a Consul Associate exam.

If you hold an unexpired Consul Associate certification: You can take the exam again starting 18 months after your previous exam date. When you pass the exam, the expiration date on your credentials will be extended. You may want to wait until Consul Associate 003 is released.

If you hold an expired Consul Associate certification: You are eligible to recertify at any time. When you pass a Consul Associate exam again, you will receive a new, separate set of credentials with a new expiration date.

HashiCorp Certified:

Consul Associate (002)

Product version tested:Consul 1.8 or higher

The Consul Associate Certification is for Site Reliability Engineers, Solutions Architects, DevOps professionals, or other Cloud Engineers who know the basic concepts and skills to build, secure, and maintain HashiCorp Consul Community. This includes understanding what enterprise features exist and what can and cannot be done using the community offering. You will be best prepared for this exam if you have professional experience using Consul in production, but performing the exam objectives in a personal demo environment may be sufficient.
